How It Works

Five steps. Zero stress.

From fee setup to real-time tracking — the whole process is smooth for everyone.

STEP
🏫
School Sets the Fees
The school administrator logs into Lopay and sets the total tuition fee for the term. Everything is configured once and ready for parents to see.
STEP
📅
Parents Choose a Plan
Parents browse the available payment schedules and pick the one that fits — weekly or monthly. No pressure, no guesswork.
STEP
💳
Parents Pay Installments
Payments are made securely through Lopay's platform. No bank queues, no stress — just a few taps from a phone.
STEP
🏦
Lopay Remits Instantly
The moment a parent makes a payment, Lopay remits it directly to the school's registered bank account. Schools never wait.
STEP
📊
Everyone Tracks in Real Time
Both parents and schools have live dashboards showing payment history, outstanding balances, and upcoming schedules.

About Lopay

Building a Nigeria where no child misses school over unpaid fees.

Lopay was born from a clear observation: millions of Nigerian parents deeply want the best education for their children, yet the pressure of a full lump-sum payment every term forces many to withdraw or delay enrollment.

We built Lopay to close that gap — a platform that makes payments flexible for parents and reliable for schools without compromise on either side.

Our Mission

To make quality education financially accessible for every Nigerian family by removing the barrier of lump-sum school fee payments — one installment at a time.

🎯
Accessibility First

Financial constraints should never be the reason a child misses school.

🤝
Trust & Transparency

Every naira tracked, every payment visible, every school paid exactly what they're owed.

⚡
Speed & Reliability

Instant remittance, real-time dashboards, and zero delays — always.
